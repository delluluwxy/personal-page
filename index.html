SeeClear: Reliable Transparent Object Depth Estimation via Generative Opacification
Xiaoying Wang*, Yumeng He*, Jingkai Shi*, Jiayin Lu, Yin Yang, Ying Jiang, Chenfanfu Jiang
* Equal contribution
Transparent objects pose unique challenges for depth estimation due to refraction and light transmission effects that confound standard sensors and neural methods. We propose a diffusion-based generative pipeline that converts transparent materials into geometrically consistent opaque shapes, enabling off-the-shelf depth estimators to produce reliable results without retraining. We also introduce SeeClear-396k, a large-scale synthetic dataset of paired transparent–opaque renderings for training.
